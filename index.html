WE GOT INNOVATION

This Physics Lab Portal is designed to streamline the management of lab activities, resources, and student engagement within Punjab College, Daewoo Road, Faisalabad. It aims to provide a centralized platform for tracking attendance, managing lab inventory for both boys and girls sections, generating comprehensive reports, and handling receipts with unparalleled efficiency. Our commitment is to foster a dynamic and organized learning environment, ensuring that our students have access to the best resources and that lab operations run seamlessly for a brighter scientific future.
